# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to engage respectfully, stay technically constructive, and avoid personal attacks or exclusionary behavior.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discussing technical disagreements in concrete terms
- giving actionable review feedback
- assuming good faith while remaining rigorous
- respecting different experience levels and backgrounds

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation
- insulting or dismissive language
- discriminatory language or behavior
- derailing discussion with personal hostility

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates these standards.
